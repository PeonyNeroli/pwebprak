Watch the exciting Quarter Final match between Faculty of Science and Technology (FST) and Faculty of Technology and Management (FTMM) in the Futsal Dies Natalies tournament. FST emerged victorious with a convincing 2-0 win.

Match Highlights:

  • 00:15 - Opening ceremony and team introductions
  • 01:32 - First half kickoff
  • 03:47 - Ahmad Rizky's amazing goal (FST) - 1:0
  • 05:22 - FTMM's missed opportunity
  • 08:15 - Second half kickoff
  • 10:43 - FST's defensive masterclass
  • 12:55 - Dimas Rahman's powerful shot (FST) - 2:0
  • 14:30 - Post-match celebrations and interviews
